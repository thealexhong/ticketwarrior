Frequently Asked Questions

How are you saving the world?

We believe that giving back to the community is a small step to take in saving the world. Thus, we have made it our mission to give back 10% of our profits to a local charity. If you would like to see us donate to a specific charity, feel free to share with us on Twitter.


How much do you charge?

We charge 40% on what we save you on your ticket. That means if we can't get your ticket dismissed or reduced you don't pay anything. For example, on a $150 ticket you save up to $90.


How is Credit Card information stored?

We do not store any credit card information on our system. We use Stripe as our online payment processor which has been audited by a PCI-certified auditor and certified to PCI Service Provider Level 1.


Do you only fight parking tickets?

Yes, but if you have other tickets we will deal with them on a case-by-case basis. Reach out to us at hello@ticketwarrior.me.
