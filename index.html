Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work?

We wanted to make the process of fighting a ticket as simple as possible. That means from when we receive your ticket we check it for errors, review it against city laws, submit a notice of intention to appear for trial, and attend trial on your behalf. We believe that everybody is innocent until proven guilty.


How much do you charge?

We charge 40% on what we save you on your ticket. For example, on a $150 ticket you save up to $90. That means if we can't get your ticket dismissed or reduced you don't pay for our service.


Great! What's next?

We are launching on April 2, 2015. In the mean time, if you have tickets for us to address feel free to email us at hello@ticketwarrior.me. Remember to sign up to get priority access!


How do I know what's happening with the ticket

Our application will send you notifications to keep you up to date on the progress of your ticket.


How are you saving the world?

We believe that giving back to the community is a small step to take in saving the world. Thus, we have made it our mission to give back 10% of our profits to a local charity. If you would like to see us donate to a specific charity, feel free to share with us on Twitter.


How is Credit Card information stored?

We do not store any credit card information on our system. We use Stripe as our online payment processor which has been audited by a PCI-certified auditor and certified to PCI Service Provider Level 1.


Do you only fight parking tickets?

Yes, but if you have other tickets we will deal with them on a case-by-case basis. Reach out to us at hello@ticketwarrior.me.
